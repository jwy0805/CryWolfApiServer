1. Items of Personal Information Collected

Hamon collects only the personal information necessary for service use and informs users in detail of the items collected.

■ Mandatory Information refers to personal information collected to provide basic services.

■ Optional Information refers to personal information collected to provide additional services beyond the basic offering.

Hamon processes the following personal information with user consent:

  • Registration: [Mandatory] ID, nickname, password
  • Payment: [Mandatory] ID
  • Refund Processing: [Mandatory] Bank account information (bank name, account holder name, account number), payment details
  • Inquiry Handling:
    • [Mandatory] Nickname, e-mail address (for non-member inquiries)
    • [Optional] ID, name, e-mail address, mobile number, date of birth, gender, address, phone number, identity verification information, payment details

To ensure service stability, prevent fraud, protect accounts and items, and restrict violations of laws or our Terms of Use, automatically generated information (IP, access logs, usage records, misuse records, download logs, payment logs, cookies) and device identifiers (OS information, hardware information, MAC Address, UUID, advertising identifiers) may be collected.

When using social login (Apple, Google, Facebook, Steam, Sony, Microsoft, etc.), only the minimum personal information agreed upon by the user is obtained and processed.

When using the YouTube API, we comply with the API Terms of Service and Google’s Privacy Policy. API access permissions can be revoked on Google’s security settings page.

2. Purposes of Processing Personal Information

Hamon uses personal information for the following purposes and will obtain separate consent or take necessary measures if purposes change:

  • Identifying and verifying members; member management
  • Verifying children under 14 and confirming legal guardian consent
  • Providing online and mobile games and related services
  • Processing payments for paid services (charging, refunds)
  • Sending event and promotion notifications
  • Delivering prizes and handling taxes
  • Notifying users of changes to terms or service disruptions
  • Handling inquiries, complaints, and service improvements
  • Ensuring service security and preventing abuse
  • Analyzing usage data and providing personalized services

6. Criteria for Additional Use or Provision of Personal Information

We consider the following criteria to determine whether to use or provide personal information additionally without consent:

  • Relevance to the original collection purpose
  • Predictability based on processing practices
  • Whether it unfairly infringes on user interests
  • Whether safety measures such as pseudonymization or encryption have been applied

7. Retention Period and Destruction of Personal Information

Personal information is destroyed without delay after the retention period expires or the purpose is achieved, and there are statutory retention periods:

  • E-commerce Act: Contract and settlement records – 5 years; Complaint and dispute records – 3 years
  • Framework Act on National Taxes & Income Tax Act: Tax-related records – 5 years
  • Communications Privacy Act: Login records – 3 months

Destruction methods: Electronic files are deleted irreversibly; paper documents are shredded or incinerated.

8. Processing of Pseudonymized Information

We pseudonymize personal information for statistical analysis, research, and public interest record-keeping, and destroy it without delay once the purpose is achieved. We implement safety measures such as separate storage, management, and record-keeping of processing activities.

9. User and Legal Representative Rights and Exercise Methods

Users may at any time request access, correction, deletion, suspension of processing, or withdrawal of consent regarding their personal information. Legal representatives may exercise the same rights for children’s personal information:

  • Via the in-game [Settings] menu for withdrawal of consent or account deletion
  • By phone or e-mail request

10. Installation, Operation, and Rejection of Automatic Personal Information Collection Devices

Hamon uses cookies to analyze access logs and usage patterns. Users may refuse cookies via browser settings, but this may limit use of some services.

We provide opt-out options for external web analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics):

  • GA Opt-out: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Behavioral information is collected for online personalized advertising; the entities collecting or processing this information include Google, Meta, X, IronSource, and others.

Users can control cookies and advertising identifiers through browser or device settings (Chrome, Edge, Whale, Android, iOS, etc.).

11. Hamon’s Efforts for Personal Information Protection
